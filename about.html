About

I'm Allison Bakalars, a GIS/Cartography, Physical Geography, History, and Environmental Studies student at UW-Madison, graduating May 2025.

My experiences are mainly with utilities and the environment, but I also do work for the History department's the Nonviolence Project. Check out my resume for further detail about my experience..

I enjoy blending my passions for GIS and infrastructure through one of my current jobs at the UW-Madison Physical Plant where I complete both in-office mapping and outdoor field collection. Through completing underground utility locates or other field work, I'm conscious about environmental impacts and the role of clean energy in our infrastructure.
